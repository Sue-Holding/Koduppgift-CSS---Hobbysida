How to get started doing your own nails at home!

So you've decided that you want to learn to do your own nails at home and avoid paying the rediculous costs at the salons.
But where do you start ???

Here is a list of a basic starter kit;

  • A UV or LED nail lamp
  • 100/180 nail files
  • Cleanser and lint free wipes
  • Top and bottom coat
  • Gel colours
  • Some nail brushes and decals/gems etc for nail art

Now you are ready to start learning

With nails it is very much practice makes perfect. So don't be disshearten if your first sets don't come out exactly as you wanted.

What to do first?

  1. First you want to buff and shape your nails.
  2. Push back your cuticles and cut them if needed.
  3. Give them a nice wipe using the cleanser.
  4. Use one coat of Bottom coat and cure under the lamp according to the cure time listed on the bottle.
  5. Choose your desired colour and paint 2-3 layers, curing between each coat.
  6. When you are happy, paint one coat of Top coat and cure.
  7. Finally give your nails a final wipe using the cleanser and you're done!
